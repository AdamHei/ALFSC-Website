Learn to Skate

The Learn To Skate Program is run by the Albert Lea Figure Skating Club in conjunction with the city of Albert Lea. Lessons are available to children ages 4 and up at any ability level. The program consists of 10 sessions starting in November and running through March. Learn To Skate members have the option to participate in a performance in the ALFSC Spring Show in March. To register for the Learn To Skate (LTS) Program, fill out the attached registration form and mail it with your payment to ALFSC, PO Box 368, Albert Lea, MN, 56007, or drop it off in the podium inside the entrance of the city arena.
