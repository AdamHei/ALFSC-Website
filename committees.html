Committees


...from the ALFSC Handbook

"The success of the AFLSC relies solely on volunteer help. This volunteer effort is not just from the board of the club but every family as well. Each family is required to volunteer for at least three projects to help the club. Because of the extreme need of things to be done, one of those projects needs to be during show week. Please be aware that some of the committees will meet several times to complete the work required. It is important that everyone volunteers to help make the current season and the annual show a success."

  • General Activities Committees

    • Banquet Planning
    • Fundraising
    • Nominating
    • Pool (Concession Stand)
    • Publicity
    • Testing (Maintain and run USFSA testing sessions)

  • Pre-Show Committees

    • Costumes
    • Music Selection
    • Props/Set
    • Publicity

  • Show Week Committees

    • Flower Sales (Preceding and during show)
    • Food Sales (During show week)
    • Prop Setup (Thursday Night and Friday of show week)
    • Prop Tear Down (After Sunday show)
    • Program and Ticket Sales (At door)
    • Room Parent (Chaperone locker room)
