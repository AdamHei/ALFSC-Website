Coach Wanted

Seeking Basic Skills/Freestyle Coach


The Albert Lea Figure Skating Club is a vibrant club with a 54-year history. The club has approximately sixty members. The ALFSC is seeking a Basic Skills/ Freestyle Coach to start this summer if possible.

This coach should follow the USFSA Basic Skills Program book, be motivating to skaters, communicate well with skaters, parents and board members, and possess all applicable certifications and insurance.

This coach will provide group classes to skaters from Tiny Tots through Freestyle Level 6. Applicant must be available 1-2 evenings per week (more during show rehearsals). Contract negotiable.

The club may also have openings for Basic Skills coaches for its Learn to Skate program, which is traditionally held on Sunday afternoons.

Submit cover letter, resume, and references by August 1st to
ALFSC
P.O. Box 368
Albert Lea, MN 56007
